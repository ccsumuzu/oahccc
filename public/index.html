Introduction

Chuck is a multidisciplinary and motivated technologist in sustainable architecture who believes that modern environmental challenges can be tackled by technology innovations and dedicated minds. He currently works as a technical consultant who develop BIM toolkit for virtual design and construction (VDC) and building performance simulation (BPS) practices in the design phase. Being an enthusiastic and result-oriented "designerd"​, Chuck also enjoys creating novel digital and data-centric methodologies, tools, and techniques for better information integrations and workflow streamlines.

With his additional 3+ years of relevant experiences in facility management, project management and planning, and energy-related policy research, he is ready to deliver excellence independently and collaboratively, and willing to immerse himself into all kinds of opportunity facilitating a better, more sustainable future of our society.
